The Lost Sheep.

The Parable of the Lost Sheep is one of the parables of Jesus. It appears in the Gospels of Matthew and Luke. It is about a shepherd who leaves his flock of ninety-nine sheep in order to find the one which is lost.

The Mustard seed.

The Parable of the Mustard Seed is one of the shorter parables of Jesus. It appears in Matthew, Mark, and Luke. In the Gospels of Matthew and Luke, it is immediately followed by the Parable of the Leaven, which shares this parable's theme of the Kingdom of Heaven growing from small beginnings.

The Barren Fig Tree

The Parable of the Barren Fig Tree is a parable of Jesus which appears in Luke 13:6-9. It is about a fig tree which does not produce fruit.
